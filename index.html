Hi, welcome to my website!

I am Nicolas Pasquier, a post-doctoral researcher in economics at Bordeaux School of Economics (BSE). I am also an associate member of Grenoble Applied Economics Lab (GAEL) where I did my PhD.

My research interests cover Industrial Organization with a focus on vertical relationships (e.g. retailing, biofuel market, digital markets, insurance markets) in the presence of uncertainty (asymmetric information, risk or ambiguity).

Please, browse this website if you wish to know more about my past and current research projects. You can also contact me at : nicolas.pasquier@univ-grenoble-alpes.fr
