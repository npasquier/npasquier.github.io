I am a Post-Doc in Economics at the Bordeaux School of Economics.

My main interests are in topics of industrial organization, particularly the strategies adopted by firms when faced with uncertainty. Recent studies cover agricultural and digital sector issues.

I am also learning software development in my spare time.
